FAQ

Can I really live in Japan without learning Japanese language?

Many people do, especially in Tokyo, where the office is located, so it is not impossible. However, the more Japanese you know, the easier it gets.

Why would I live in Tokyo?

If you are like me who really appreciates cleanliness, organization, safety and convenience, Tokyo is the place for you. Besides the amazingly cheap and delicious japanese food, extremely wide range of activities to do from bowling to tennis, from shopping for anime/manga goods to going to maid cafes, from the beautiful pink flower blossoms in Spring to the gorgeous red leaves in Autumn, Tokyo is the place to go.

How about the famous long overtime work system in Japan?

In our company we encourage people to work just the normal 8 hours a day. We have a flex time from 9am to 6pm of one hour, which means you can arrive between 8am and 10am and work 8 hours from there. If you come at 8am and leave by 5pm, no one will think less of you in our company.
